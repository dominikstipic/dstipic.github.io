Zeitgeist



Description

The Zeitgeist is the startup which aims to create EduTech solution for cognitive training and enchancing your mental processes and providing guided eruditon improvement by making targeted and personalized learning outcomes which could be applied universaly.

Mission

The company's mission is to create an application system to improve cognitive processes, listening skills and other mental processes and connect these processes with universal business activities and creative and personalized incentive options. Communication, listening skills, vocabulary and poetry are tremendously important in business and life. For most people these skills are learned by exposure to the environment, reading, watching and mimesis. Modern professional environments converge to the point where single high-quality threads of thought are immediately executable and put into action. The Zeitgeist company commits itself to creating a technological system which could change the education and training of the humans to create a better version of them.

Product

Product Mneme is a proof of concept project which aims to mimic the communcation process and help people develop their soft skills by talkig with the mobile phone. Analytics and data science allows user to analyse his performance, memory and his body language while talking. The technology is founded on the latest natural language processing development to simulate talking and listening coaches.
