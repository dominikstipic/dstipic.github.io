About Me

    Hello Everyone,
    My name is Dominik Stipić and welcome to my blog. I graduated from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering in Zagreb. You can find more about my career path in my CV which I provided in the links below. I'm a technologist who possesses a wide range of interests. I like to study modern technology, the decision-making process, quantitative methods, mathematics, computer science, and socio-technological aspects of how technology shapes the economy and how it affects human relationships and behaviour. As you can see I have a wide range of interests and this blog serves me the purpose of putting "on paper" the ideas which pop up in my mind and what I am currently researching or reading. I'm interested in information technologies, business intelligence and analytic field applied to financial and social sciences. I'm big supported of the data-dependent decision making process approach combined with the Bayesian prior where one doesn't have enough evidence. In my free time, I like to travel, exercise, run, read, explore new ideas and organize my life. If you want to contact me or to propose some collaboration please contact me via email or phone.
